# Lucky Spin

Try your luck with the Wheel of Fortune!

## How It Works

1. Go to Lucky Spin page
2. Pay the spin cost (XPET)
3. Spin the wheel
4. Win a random prize!

## Prizes

The wheel contains various prizes:
- XPET tokens (different amounts)
- Bonus multipliers
- Special rewards
- Jackpot prizes

## Spin Cost

Each spin costs a small amount of XPET. The cost is displayed before you spin.

## Tips

- Only spin what you can afford to lose
- Lucky Spin is entertainment, not guaranteed profit
- Bigger prizes have lower chances
- Have fun and enjoy the thrill!

## Fairness

All spins use a random number generator to ensure fair results. Each prize has a set probability that doesn't change based on previous spins.
