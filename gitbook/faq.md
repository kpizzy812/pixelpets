# FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Pixel Pets.

## General

### What is Pixel Pets?
Pixel Pets is a Telegram Mini App where you buy virtual pets, train them daily, and earn XPET rewards.

### Is it free to play?
You need XPET to buy pets and start earning. There's no free-to-play option currently.

### What is XPET?
XPET is the in-game currency. 1 XPET = $1 USD.

## Pets

### How many pets can I own?
You can have up to 3 pets at once (3 slots).

### Which pet should I buy first?
Start with what you can afford. Common pets are cheapest but have lower daily rates. Higher tier pets earn more but cost more.

### What happens when my pet reaches ROI cap?
The pet stops earning and moves to your Hall of Fame. The slot becomes available for a new pet.

### Can I sell my pet?
Yes! You can sell any pet for 85% of the invested amount.

## Training

### How long is a training session?
Each training session takes exactly 24 hours.

### Do I need to be online during training?
No. Training happens automatically. Just come back after 24 hours to claim.

### What if I don't claim immediately?
Your rewards wait for you. There's no penalty for claiming late.

## Wallet

### How do I deposit?
Go to Wallet → Deposit → Send USDT to the provided address.

### How do I withdraw?
Go to Wallet → Withdraw → Enter amount and your wallet address.

### How long do withdrawals take?
Withdrawal processing time may vary. Check the app for current status.

## Referrals

### How do I get my referral link?
Go to Referrals page and copy your unique link.

### When do I get referral rewards?
You earn when your referrals claim their training rewards.

### Why can't I see Level 2+ commissions?
Higher referral levels unlock based on your number of active referrals.

## Support

### I have a problem. Where can I get help?
Contact our support through the Telegram bot or community channel.

### I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please report bugs through our official Telegram channel or support.
