# Pixel Pets

Welcome to Pixel Pets — an NFT-style economic game on Telegram where you buy pixel pets, train them daily, and earn rewards!

## What is Pixel Pets?

Pixel Pets is a Telegram Mini App game. You purchase virtual pets with XPET tokens, train them every day, and collect earnings. Pets grow from Baby to Mythic level, and when they reach their maximum earnings — they enter the Hall of Fame.

## Quick Start

1. Open the bot in Telegram
2. Top up your XPET balance
3. Buy your first pet
4. Start training (24 hours)
5. Claim your reward and repeat!
