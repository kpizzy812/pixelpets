# Pets

Your pets are your main source of income in Pixel Pets. Each pet has unique characteristics that determine how much you can earn.

## Pet Types

| Pet | Price | Daily Rate | ROI Cap | Max Earnings |
|-----|-------|------------|---------|--------------|
| Common | 100 XPET | 1.0% | 150% | 150 XPET |
| Uncommon | 500 XPET | 1.5% | 160% | 800 XPET |
| Rare | 1,000 XPET | 1.8% | 170% | 1,700 XPET |
| Epic | 5,000 XPET | 2.0% | 180% | 9,000 XPET |
| Legendary | 10,000 XPET | 2.5% | 200% | 20,000 XPET |

## Pet Levels

Each pet can evolve through 3 levels:

### Baby
- Starting level for all pets
- Base daily rate applies

### Adult
- Upgraded version with better earnings
- Requires upgrade fee

### Mythic
- Maximum level
- Best daily rate
- After reaching ROI cap, pet enters Hall of Fame

## Pet Slots

You have **3 pet slots** available. This means you can own and train up to 3 pets simultaneously.

## Pet States

| State | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Idle | Pet is waiting, not training |
| Training | Pet is in 24-hour training session |
| Ready to Claim | Training complete, rewards available |
| Evolved | Pet reached ROI cap and moved to Hall of Fame |
| Sold | Pet was sold back to the game |

## How to Buy a Pet

1. Go to the Shop
2. Choose a pet type you can afford
3. Click "Buy"
4. Pet appears in your slots

## Upgrading Pets

To upgrade a pet to the next level:

1. Select your pet
2. Click "Upgrade"
3. Pay the upgrade fee
4. Pet evolves to the next level with better earnings
